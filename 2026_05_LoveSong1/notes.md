What love songs are famous and what do I like?

10 of the Most Famous Love Songs

“I Will Always Love You” – Whitney Houston (1992)
A powerhouse ballad and one of the best-selling love songs ever.

“My Heart Will Go On” – Celine Dion (1997)
The unforgettable theme from Titanic.

“Unchained Melody” – The Righteous Brothers (1965)
A classic often associated with deep, enduring love.

“Endless Love” – Diana Ross & Lionel Richie (1981)
One of the most famous duets in pop history.

“Something” – The Beatles (1969)
George Harrison’s timeless and widely praised love song.

“Can’t Help Falling in Love” – Elvis Presley (1961)
A gentle, enduring romantic classic.

“Make You Feel My Love” – Bob Dylan (1997) / Adele (2008)
Covered many times—Adele’s version is especially popular.

“Perfect” – Ed Sheeran (2017)
A modern wedding favorite.

“All of Me” – John Legend (2013)
A heartfelt piano ballad dedicated to his wife.

“How Deep Is Your Love” – Bee Gees (1977)
A smooth, soulful expression of devotion.
